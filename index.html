About Me

My name is Julie Diaz and I am a junior at Hunter College studying emerging media. I was born and raised in Queens New York and I love to create new art. I particularly love animation, stop motion, and claymation. These mediums allow me to explore the world in a more fantastical way, but at the same time grounding you in a reality that could be possible if you pursue your dreams. I hope to pursue children's animation because it pushes you to think creatively and be more imaginative, as a child would. Animation allows you to push the limits of the real world and have a magical experience. I hope to one day tell the stories of people like me and inspire young women like myself.
